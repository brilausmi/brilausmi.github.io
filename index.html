ABOUT

My name is Brittany Smith.

I am a junior at Central Michigan University, majoring in Graphic Design. I focus on web/mobile design, logo design, typography, and illustration. Basically, I love to create, whether it be digital or traditional media. If I'm not in the process of making something, then I'm most likely either spending time with my family, my dogs, or playing video games.

I think that there is aesthetic beauty in most everything. I like to be experimental with my work, and I strive to stand out from the crowd with my designs, while at the same time maintaining a contemporary style. I am largely inspired by nature, specifically the colors, sounds, patterns, and vibes of nature. I also find inspiration in music, as well as a reaction to my personal life around me.


You can follow me on my personal twitter or Instagram to see what's up in my daily life.
